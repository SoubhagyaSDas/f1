The realm of F1

What is Formula One?

For some it's super complex machines designed to go fastest round a circuit, for some it is nothing more than a regular motorsport, just with some big brands and sponsers, but to really know F1 you have to visualise yourself strapped to your toilet seat going faster than you could blink and braking at speeds fighter pilots lose their consiousness.

Before commenting on Formula One as a sport, lets talk about every sport of common interest, not that you don't know what football or soccer is but just for the sake of reference, lets think like what the game of football actually is! Thinking from your perspective, isn't it 22 players trying desperately to kick a ball into two designated nets? Isn't Boxing a lame concept of two guys never known to each other, trying their best to knock each other out as hard as possible? A game viewed with the purest of intent can never fail to interest you and just like that game, F1 for millions is the purest form of engineering syncronisation and hardwork.

Technology Stripped to it's bare bones, that's what F1 truely is.
